What We Do

Human Factors & Security

Understanding how people actually think, behave, and make decisions in digital environments — and designing security that works with human behaviour, not against it.

Privacy by Design

Embedding privacy from the ground up, not as an afterthought. Clear purpose, proportionate controls, and governance that stands up to scrutiny.

Digital Identity

Human-centred approaches to identity, authentication and trust — balancing usability, security and societal impact.

Responsible AI & Policy

Ethics, governance and oversight for AI systems, with a focus on positive social outcomes and long-term public trust. Advising regulators, standards bodies and public sector organisations on the human dimensions of digital risk.

Our Approach

Digital systems do not fail only for technical reasons.

They fail when human behaviour, organisational culture and social context are ignored.

Skerry Digital works at the intersection of people, security and trust, bringing clarity, realism and principled judgement to complex digital challenges.

Our focus is not scale or spectacle, but sound foundations that hold under pressure.
